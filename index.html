Empowering Young Minds

Building the Future

At the Taye Olutimehin Foundation, we are dedicated to supporting students with scholarships and education grants that inspire excellence and academic growth. We believe that education is the strongest tool for empowerment, and we strive to create equal opportunities for all students to reach their full potential.

Through our scholarship, bursary and training programs, we nurture talented young minds, encourage hard work, and promote a culture of learning and service. Our goal is to build a generation of educated, responsible, and visionary leaders who will contribute positively to society and the world at large.

About Us

Discover our journey, mission, and the passion driving our educational initiatives.

Our Story

Taye Olutimehin Foundation was founded in 2023 out of passion for the less privileged, by Mr. Taye Olutimehin (FCAI) to support Nigerian youths through education.

We have provided scholarships to almost 20 secondary and higher institution students in Abuja, Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria being our registered head office to transform lives and building a brighter future for our nation.

Our Vision

"A Nigeria where every child has access to quality education regardless of their background."

We believe that education is the cornerstone of personal development and national progress, and we are committed to breaking down barriers that prevent talented students from reaching their full potential.

Through strategic partnerships with educational institutions, corporate sponsors, and passionate volunteers, we continue to expand our impact and affect more lives positively each year. Our comprehensive scholarship programs cover various educational levels, from secondary school to postgraduate studies, ensuring that no deserving student is left behind due to financial constraints.
